aboutabout myself

I’m an innovative software engineer and designer with almost two decades of experience managing all aspects of the development process for private companies, non-profit organizations, as well as federal government projects.

Born in Connecticut in 1975, I grew up in the DC area with a strong passion for drawing and design. After graduating from Georgetown University In 1998 on a academic scholarship, I began as a graphic artist for my father’s publishing company Writers’ Inc - putting my art minor to full use. After a couple of years of design-focused work, I came to the realization that with a solid understanding of web programming, the possibilities were endless. Equipped with programming knowledge from classes at Georgetown and a strong interest in the world wide web, I started developing high impact websites for various companies and organizations.


One of the highlights of my career was creating award-winning web and Flash applications for NASA, Software AG, Online Resources and many more. I also learned more about server side programming (PHP, ASP.NET) databases, (MySQL, SQL Server). Around 2009, when Adobe Flash started to become obsolete, my focus turned to JavaScript, HTML5 and CSS, developing and maintaining websites for various companies throughout my career.


When mobile devices became more and more popular in 2010, I began developing cross platform applications using JavaScript. To date, I have developed 6 cross platform apps and led teams in the development of powerful web applications using the latest Javascript libraries (Angular, React, React Native, Expo, etc) for organizations and companies such as Accenture, NASA, The Great Courses, etc.
